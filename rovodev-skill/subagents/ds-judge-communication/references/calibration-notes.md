# Communication Judge Calibration Notes

## Cross-Dimension Guidance

- All communication dimensions use numeric 1-10 scoring. Anchor to the rubric, not your general impression.
- A 7 is genuinely good. Most first-draft analyses score 5-7 on communication.
- Score each dimension independently. Strong narrative flow does not excuse a missing exec summary.

## Common Calibration Errors

1. **Halo effect:** A polished writing style inflates all communication scores. A beautifully written analysis with no exec summary still scores 1-2 on executive_summary_clarity.
2. **N/A inflation:** visualization_effectiveness should be 7.0 when no visualizations exist, not 9 or 10.
3. **Format bias:** Bullet points and tables are not inherently better than prose. Score the communication, not the formatting.

## Output Consistency

Always return all 4 dimensions plus critique. Never omit a dimension.
