# Methodology Soundness (Numeric 1-10)

Score this dimension on a 1-10 scale using the anchors below. Be critical but fair. A 7 is genuinely good work. A 9 is exceptional.

## What to evaluate

Is the analytical approach clearly described and appropriate for the question? Are assumptions stated explicitly? Are limitations acknowledged? Could another analyst reproduce this work?

## Scoring Anchors

- **9-10:** Method fully reproducible; assumptions explicit; limitations discussed with mitigation strategies
- **7-8:** Method mostly clear; most assumptions stated; some limitations noted
- **5-6:** Method described but gaps in reproducibility; implicit assumptions the reader must guess
- **3-4:** Method vague; major assumptions unstated; another analyst could not reproduce
- **1-2:** No methodology section or completely inadequate

## What moves the score up

- Naming the specific analytical method (e.g., "Bayesian Structural Time Series" not just "time series analysis")
- Stating assumptions as explicit numbered items
- A limitations section that goes beyond generic caveats to discuss THIS analysis's specific weaknesses
- Providing enough detail (data sources, filters, time windows, parameters) for reproduction

## What moves the score down

- "We analyzed the data" with no method description
- Assumptions the reader must infer from context
- Claiming reproducibility without providing parameters or data sources
- Methodology that contradicts itself (e.g., claiming MoM but using overlapping windows)
